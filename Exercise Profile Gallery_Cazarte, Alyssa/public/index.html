Main Characters of All Of Us Are Dead

MEET THEM INDIVIDUALLY!

Synopsis:

After a failed science experiment, a local high school is overrun with zombies, and the trapped students struggle to survive. With no food or water, and

communication cut-off by the government, they must use equipment around the school to protect themselves in the midst of a battleground or they will

become part of the infected.
